# Zenities Wallpapers Collection

A curated collection of wallpapers for my Arch Linux setup. I don't own any of these artworks—I'm just a fan sharing the aesthetic.

---

## Credits & Attribution
Most of these images were sourced from various corners of the internet. I believe in giving credit where it's due:

* **Artist Name:** The filename of each image corresponds to the artist's name. Please check them out and support their work!
* **The "?" Files:** Files marked with a `?` are images where I couldn't find the original source. 

### Missing Credits?
If you see an image marked with a `?` and you know the artist (or if you are the artist), please **open an Issue**. I would be more than happy to add the proper credit.

### Take-down Policy
I respect the rights of digital artists. If you own any of the artwork in this repository and do not consent to it being shared here, please:
* Open an **Issue** in this repository.

**I will remove the content immediately upon request.**
